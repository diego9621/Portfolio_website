Automated Business Intelligence Pipeline

Built a comprehensive automated reporting system that transforms raw business data into actionable insights, delivering daily KPI reports with sophisticated year-over-year analysis directly to team communication channels.

The system leverages BigQuery's powerful analytics capabilities to process complex sales data, calculate YoY growth metrics, and identify top-performing customers with intelligent data enrichment and URL normalization.

Advanced SQL Analytics

Year-over-Year Comparisons

Complex SQL queries calculate precise YoY metrics using DATE_SUB with 52-week intervals to compare the same weekday from the previous year, ensuring accurate seasonal trend analysis.

DATE_SUB(DATE_SUB(CURRENT_DATE(), INTERVAL 1 DAY), INTERVAL 52 WEEK)
